Privacy Policy

Smart Genie ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage statistics to improve app features (e.g., AI query counts, feature usage frequency).
  • AI Interactions: Text and images sent to AI features are processed by third-party AI providers (Google Gemini). We do not permanently store your AI conversation content on our servers.
  • Purchase Information: For premium subscriptions, Google Play handles all payment processing. We store only the purchase status, not payment details.
  • Advertising Data: For non-premium users, Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our AI-powered utility features
  • To manage your account and premium subscription status
  • To display relevant advertisements (non-premium users only)
  • To analyze app usage patterns for improvement

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS) with encryption in transit and at rest. Firebase Authentication handles secure sign-in. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and analytics
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (non-premium users)
  • Google Gemini AI: AI processing
  • Supabase: Database and backend services
  • Google Play Billing: In-app purchases

Your Rights

  • Data Access: You can view your data within the app settings.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Smart Genie is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary. You can request account deletion at any time, which will permanently remove your data from our active databases.
